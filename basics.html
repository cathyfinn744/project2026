this is the basics saftey and form page Safety and good form are your foundation. They protect your body and build genuine strength. Warm up for 5–10 minutes before lifting. Maintain natural posture—avoid rounding your back or locking joints. Breathe properly: exhale on effort, inhale on return. Start light, progress gradually. There’s no rush. Ask for a spotter if lifting heavy or trying new movements. Listen to your body—pain means stop, discomfort can mean growth
